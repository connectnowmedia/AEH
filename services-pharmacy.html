Pharmacy

Annapoorna Pharmacy is a well-stocked pharmacy located within the premises of Annapoorna Eye Hospital. Our pharmacy is designed to provide patients with easy access to the medications and supplements they need to maintain their health and well-being. We offer a wide range of prescription and non-prescription medications, as well as vitamins and supplements, to help our patients manage their conditions and prevent disease. Our knowledgeable pharmacists are always available to answer any questions patients may have about their medications and provide guidance on the proper use and storage of these medications. At Annapoorna Pharmacy, we are committed to providing high-quality, affordable medications and excellent customer service to all our patients.
